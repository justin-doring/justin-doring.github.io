Sixers Completed The Sweep Of The Brooklyn Nets With a 96-88 Win

The Sixers completed the sweep of the Nets on the 22nd of April in game 4, without MVP candidate Joel Embiid. The Sixers will go on to face the winner of the Celtics-Hawks series in the Eastern Conference Semi-Finals.

Joel Embiid To Miss Game 4 Against The Brooklyn Nets

Joel Embiid suffered a sprained right knee during the Sixers' game 3 win over the Nets. Embiid will miss game 4 but there is optimism that he can be ready to return as early as next week.
